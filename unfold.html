Unfold Hands started as a small local gathering in Jasho Farm. Today, we are a vital support system ensuring that every child, regardless of their background, has access to basic rights and a chance to thrive.

Our Core Programs

Comprehensive care focused on the physical, mental, and educational needs of children.

Education Plus

Providing school kits, uniforms, and covering tuition gaps for vulnerable primary students.

Health & Nutrition

Weekly meal programs and medical advocacy to ensure no child in Jasho Farm goes hungry.

Family Outreach

Empowering parents with skills and counseling to create safer environments for children.
